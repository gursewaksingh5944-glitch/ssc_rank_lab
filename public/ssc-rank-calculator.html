How to Calculate Your SSC Rank from Marks

Are you preparing for SSC exams like CGL, CHSL, or MTS? Knowing your expected rank based on marks is crucial for career planning. Our guide explains the SSC rank calculation process and offers a free tool to estimate your position.

Why calculate rank? SSC ranks determine your eligibility for posts. Higher ranks mean better job opportunities.

SSC Rank Calculation Method

SSC ranks are calculated based on:

  • Normalized Marks: Adjusted for paper difficulty.
  • Total Candidates: Number of applicants.
  • Cutoff Scores: Minimum marks for qualification.

Formula: Rank = (Total Qualified Candidates + 1) - Your Position in Merit List

Example: If 10,000 qualify and you score higher than 8,000, your rank is ~2,001.
