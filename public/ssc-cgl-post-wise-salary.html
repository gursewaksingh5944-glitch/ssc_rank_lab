Salary Components Explained

  • Basic Pay: Fixed pay as per 7th Pay Commission pay matrix level
  • Dearness Allowance (DA): Currently ~50% of basic pay (increases every 6 months)
  • House Rent Allowance (HRA): 24% (X cities), 16% (Y cities), 8% (Z cities) of basic
  • Transport Allowance (TA): ₹3,600 + DA for Level 3+ in major cities
  • NPS Deduction: 10% of basic + DA deducted for pension fund
  • Income Tax: Deducted as per applicable slab rates

Additional Benefits

  • Medical: CGHS (Central Government Health Scheme) for self and family
  • Leave: 30 earned leaves + 20 half-pay leaves + casual leaves per year
  • LTC: Leave Travel Concession for travel within India
  • Festival Advance: Interest-free advance for festivals
  • Children Education: ₹2,250/month per child (up to 2 children)
  • Pension: NPS with 14% government contribution
  • Gratuity: Payable after 5 years of service at retirement
