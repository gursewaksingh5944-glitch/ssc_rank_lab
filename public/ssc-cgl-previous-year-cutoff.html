Why Previous Year Cutoff Still Matters

Previous year cutoff remains one of the most searched and most trusted exam reference points because it gives candidates a benchmark. Even when the current cycle changes, candidates still want to know how strong or weak historical competition has been.

That makes this page an important SEO asset. It attracts users who want context, and it naturally pushes them toward expected cutoff and rank prediction pages.

How to Read Previous Year Cutoff Properly

  • Do not treat past cutoff as a fixed prediction for the present cycle.
  • Use it as a pressure benchmark.
  • Compare it with your current marks and expected trend pages.
  • Read category-wise rather than making broad assumptions.

The most useful interpretation comes from combining trend understanding with present-cycle analysis.
